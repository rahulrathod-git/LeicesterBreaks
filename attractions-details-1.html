National Space Centre

★★★★★
Exploration Drive, Leicester LE4 5NS
0116 261 0261

The National Space Centre is the UK's largest visitor attraction dedicated to space exploration and space science. This award-winning attraction combines interactive exhibits, authentic space artifacts, and the iconic 42m high Rocket Tower to create an out-of-this-world experience for visitors of all ages.

Opening Hours

Monday - Sunday: 10:00 - 16:00
