Bradgate Park

★★★★★
Newtown Linford, Leicester LE6 0HE
0116 236 2713

Bradgate Park is a stunning 850-acre park, known for its roaming red and fallow deer, ancient woodlands, and rugged landscapes. Highlights include the dramatic ruins of Bradgate House and the historic Old John Tower, both set against a backdrop of breathtaking natural beauty.

Reviews

Senna ★★★★★

A beautiful natural escape near the city. The deer roam freely and are a delight to observe. The ruins of Bradgate House provide a fascinating historical backdrop. Perfect for a family day out.

Emma ★★★★★

Stunning park with great walking trails. The views from Old John Tower are spectacular on a clear day. I love visiting in different seasons as the landscape changes dramatically. Autumn is particularly beautiful with the changing colors.

Dennis ★★★★★

One of Leicestershire's true gems. Great place for walking, wildlife spotting and taking photos. The ancient oak trees are amazing and there's plenty of space so it never feels overcrowded even on busy days.
